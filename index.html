About Us

We are a licensed, caring family home daycare in Boston, dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where your child can thrive socially, emotionally, and academically. Our small group sizes ensure personalized attention for every little star.

Programs & Age Groups

Infant Care (6 weeks - 18 months)

Focus on foundational cognitive and motor skills through sensory play and consistent, loving interaction.

Toddler Explorers (18 months - 3 years)

Encouraging independence, language development, and social skills through guided play and exploration.

Pre-K Prep (3 years - 5 years)

Structured activities focusing on early literacy, numeracy, and school readiness in a fun, positive setting.
